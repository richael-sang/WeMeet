  2. Find the booking you want to cancel
  3. Click the "Cancel" button
  4. Confirm the cancellation

Note: Cancellations within 24 hours of the booking start time may be restricted

Can I book multiple meeting rooms at the same time?
Yes, but please avoid booking multiple rooms for the same time slot unnecessarily.
What are the different booking statuses?
Booking statuses include: Approved, Rejected, and Cancelled.
What should I do if my booking is rejected?
You can check the rejection reason and try booking a different room or time slot.
